Staff Job Management

A guide to posting and managing job listings.

What You Can Post

  • Batch: e.g., "2025"
  • Job Description: Short job details
  • Department: e.g., "Computer Science, IT" (comma-separated)
  • Drive Link: URL to details (e.g., Google Drive)
  • Expiration: Deadline (e.g., "12/31/2025 23:59:59")
  • Company Name: Company offering the job
  • Drive Date: e.g., "12/31/2025"

Track jobs and student registrations by department or batch.

How to Post

  1. Go to "Create a New Job Post."
  2. Fill in: Batch, Description, Departments, Link, Expiration, Company, Drive Date.
  3. Click "Create Job."
  4. Success? See confirmation. Errors? Fix and retry.

Managing Jobs

  • View Details: Click "Show Details" for student info.
  • Remove: Click "Remove" to delete a job.
  • Full List: Click "Select for Full List" for details.
  • Refresh: Click "Refresh Job List" to update.

Tips

  • Use formats: MM/DD/YYYY for Drive Date, MM/DD/YYYY HH:MM:SS for Expiration.
  • Ensure links are valid.
  • Check error messages if issues arise.
